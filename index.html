About Cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players each on a field with a pitch at the center. It's popular worldwide, especially in England, India, Australia, and Pakistan.

Basic Rules

  • The game is played in innings, where one team bats and the other fields.
  • Objective: Score more runs than the opposing team.
  • Formats: Test (up to 5 days), ODI (50 overs), T20 (20 overs).

Famous Players
